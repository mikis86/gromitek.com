About Gromitek

An independent automation engineering practice built on 15 years of on-site experience.

Founder & Senior Automation Engineer

Gromitek is Miloš Slijepčević — founder, general manager and senior automation engineer, all in one. Over 15 years in software development and plant commissioning & start-up for the industrial automation sector, with a focus on the steel making industry — long products.

Most of that time has been spent on site: electrical erection and pre-commissioning support, cold and hot commissioning, debugging, and production assistance during ramp-up. The rest has gone into developing standard and client-customized automation software for specific line types, including rolling mill, spooler, twin channel, revolver line, wire rod and apron line.

Gromitek exists to bring that same depth of hands-on, on-site engineering to clients directly — without the overhead of a large organization.
